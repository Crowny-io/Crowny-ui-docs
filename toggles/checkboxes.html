Important

The crowny__toggles comes default with the Crowny-UI library and is needed for the custom checkbox positioning.

# Markup

<div class="crowny__toggles">
    <input id="checkbox-1" type="checkbox" value="Check me!" checked>
    <label for="checkbox-1" class="checkbox">Check me!</label>
    <input id="checkbox-2" type="checkbox" value="Me too!">
    <label for="checkbox-2" class="checkbox">Me too!</label>  
</div>
