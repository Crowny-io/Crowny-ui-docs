Switches #

Default switches #

Switches are basically checkboxes under the hood but with a different skin.

Preview #

Important

The crowny__toggles comes default with the Crowny-UI library and is needed for the custom checkbox positioning.

Markup #

<div class="crowny__toggles">
    <input id="checkbox-7" type="checkbox" value="Turn on the lights!">
    <label for="checkbox-7" class="switch round checkbox">Turn on the lights!</label>
    <input id="checkbox-8" type="checkbox" value="Turn on the lambo!" checked>
    <label for="checkbox-8" class="switch round checkbox">Turn on the lambo!</label> 
</div>
